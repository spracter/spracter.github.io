Information Collection And Use
We do not collect any private information about our users. Your notes or any content that you store on the app is never collected or transferred anywhere in any form. We only collect data related to any transcations or purchases you make towards our services from within the app. This data is limited to the order ID and generic receipt data which helps us track orders and serve our users better. Storage of data
We store your data on your device in a secure database. A copy of your data is also stored safely in form of a secure backup online on your personal Google Drive. This data is never transmitted to anyone or shared in any form and is only available to you.

Our apps use and store data for these purposes:

Your information, including any personal data, notes, settings or passcodes is never transferred anywhere and is solely stored on your device and online in your personal Google Drive. Your consent to this Privacy Policy represents your agreement to these uses. Security Of Data
Your data is as secure as the security measures put in place by your device's operating system and by Google Drive for online backups. The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
