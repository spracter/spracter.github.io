Memiq | Privacy Policy
Effective date: Aug 15, 2020

Spracter (and Spracter GameDivision) ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Memiq and other mobile applications (the "Service") made/developed/owned by Spracter (and Spracter GameDivision).

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection And Use
We do not collect any private information about our users. Any data that can be used to personally identify you is never collected or transferred anywhere in any form. We only collect the following data:
  1. Email: Your email is needed to uniquely identify you.
  2. Anonymous Game Usage Data: Data related to normal game/app usage to help improve our product and make informed decisions in the future for a better user experience. This data cannot be used to personally identify any player in any way or form. We collect this data on the basis of a legitimate interest in needs-oriented design, for statistical evaluation, efficient marketing of our app, and the fact that your legitimate interests do not prevail under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  3. Transactions: Data about any transcations or purchases you make towards our services from within the app/game. This data is limited to the order ID and generic receipt data which helps us track orders and serve our users better. It does not include any private data of the user like card numbers or passwords.
  4. In-game Player Profile Data: Anonymous data like username & country that is used to maintain a player profile inside the game, which is visible and accessible to all other players across the world.

Apart from the data mentioned above, we use the some advertising networks, mainly Google AdMob in order to be able to integrate advertising within our free-of-charge game. These networks collect the data with the purpose of providing personalized advertising. The privacy policies of these networks are separate from ours and, in no way are we associated or involved with their rules of privacy policies. The privacy policy for Google and their products and services can be found here.
You can object to the use of personalized advertising by making the following settings: First open “Google Settings,” tap on “Advertising,” then open “Disable interest-related ads,” then tap on “OK.” Use of the aforementioned advertising network(s) and the transmission of the data mentioned is based on our legitimate interest in recouping our investments for operation of our app through advertising and the fact that your justified interests are not overriding, Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Right to information
Even though we do not collect any personal data about you, including any passwords, you are entitled at any time, upon request, to obtain information from us about your personal data within the scope of Article 15 GDPR. To request such information you may submit an application by email to support@spracter.com

Support requests
If you send support requests to us by email, via chat within the game or via messages within social media platforms, we collect and process the data from your request in order to be able to provide a response. For this purpose, it may also be necessary for you to provide us with information about your end device so that we can process the request and resolve any technical problems that may exist. We collect this data in order to provide our app to you and to be able to process your request, Article 6(1)(b) GDPR. You are not required to provide the above voluntary information. In particular, you are free to send the request to us under a pseudonym, without giving your real name.

Storage of data
We store your data (player profile) on your device in a secure database. A copy of this data is also stored on our secure servers online, which is required for the basic functioning of the game/app. This data does not contain any information that can be used to personally identify you. This data is accessible by all the players/users of the game/app but is only modifiable by you. This data does not include your email, which is kept separately in another secure database for the purpose of authentication and this database is not accessible by anyone directly.

Use of Data
Our game/app use and store data for these purposes:
  1. To provide and maintain the Service.
  2. To assist you in cases where you need help.
  3. To track your orders so as to ensure seamless and uninterrupted services.
  4. To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so.

Transfer Of Data
Spracter (and Spracter GameDivision) will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of your Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of your data and other personal information.

Security Of Data
Your data is as secure as the security measures put in place by your device's operating system and by Google's robust servers and database services for online storage. The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes To This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes through within the application. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
